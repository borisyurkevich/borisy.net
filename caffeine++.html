Caffeine++

Information is Power.

Track your caffeine and water consumption to gain more personal data.

Caffeine++ became much better since 1.0 release. Our goal is to improve and we couldn't do it without help from our users.

  • Contribute data to Apple Health
  • Add any amount of your favorite presets for quick logging
  • Rearrange with drag and drop.
  • Use Apple Watch
  • Add your own drinks
  • Customize entry date
  • Water tracking
  • Supports milliliters, fluid ounces and imperial fluid ounces

Please limit your caffeine intake to 200 mg if you are pregnant.
