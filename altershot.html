Privacy Policy

Altershot asks for Photos permission. Access is required for copying all screenshots from Photos to the Altershot app. User is in full control of data. Altershot doesn't have any servers. The data will be stored only on device, unless user will take some action on it, for example sending email or iMessage.

We may store your email address for some time if you send support email to us within the app.
